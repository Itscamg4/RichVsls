Terms & Conditions

Booking & Cancellation Policy — RichVsls

1. Booking & Deposits

  • Deposits: A non-refundable deposit is required at the time of booking to secure your session date. Deposits range from $35–$60 depending on the package selected.
  • Balance: Remaining balance is due at the time of your session.
  • Confirmation: Your booking is not confirmed until your deposit has been received.

2. Cancellations & Rescheduling

  • Client Cancellation: Cancellations 14+ days before the session may be eligible for a reschedule. Retainer is non-refundable.
  • Rescheduling: One reschedule permitted at no charge with 7 days' notice.

3. Delivery of Images

  • Turnaround: Edited galleries are delivered within 1–2 days of your session.
  • Additional Edits: Extra edits beyond the included count are available at $5 per edit.
  • Raw Photos: All raw (unedited) photos are included with every package.
  • Gallery Access: Online galleries are available for 60 days from delivery. Download all images within this window.

4. Image Rights & Usage

  • Client Usage: Upon full payment, clients receive personal print rights for all delivered images.
  • Commercial Use: Must be agreed upon in writing prior to the session.
  • Copyright: RichVsls retains full copyright of all images produced.
